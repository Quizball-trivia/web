# QuizBall - Project Description

## Executive Summary

**QuizBall** is a next-generation multiplayer football (soccer) trivia gaming platform that combines competitive gameplay, social interaction, and gamification to create an engaging experience for football fans worldwide. Available on mobile (iOS/Android) and web, QuizBall offers multiple game modes, real-time multiplayer battles, daily challenges, and a comprehensive progression system with achievements, levels, and rewards.

**Mission:** To create the ultimate football trivia experience that brings fans together through competitive knowledge-based gameplay while celebrating the beautiful game.

**Vision:** To become the #1 football trivia platform globally, with millions of active players engaging daily across multiple game modes and building a vibrant community of football enthusiasts.

---

## Project Overview

### What is QuizBall?

QuizBall is a multiplayer-only football trivia game that tests players' knowledge of football history, players, teams, tactics, competitions, and memorable moments. Unlike traditional single-player quiz apps, QuizBall focuses exclusively on competitive multiplayer experiences where players face off against opponents from around the world in real-time battles.

### Core Concept

Players compete in fast-paced trivia matches, earning coins, XP, and climbing global leaderboards. The game features a daily ticket system (10 tickets per 24 hours) that encourages regular engagement without overwhelming players. With multiple game modes ranging from 1v1 ranked matches to 10-player buzzer battles, QuizBall offers variety and replayability while maintaining competitive integrity through its ranking system.

### Platform Availability

- **Mobile App:** Native iOS and Android applications
- **Web App:** Responsive web application accessible from any modern browser
- **Cross-Platform:** Shared backend, synchronized progress across all platforms

## Key Features

### 1. Game Modes

#### QuizBall (1v1 Ranked Mode)
- Real-time 1v1 matches with ranked matchmaking
- Category-based questions (Premier League, Champions League, World Cup, etc.)
- 10 questions per match with time-based scoring
- Rank progression system with visible opponent matching
- Live opponent status during gameplay

#### Buzzer Battle (Multiplayer 2-10 Players)
- Fast-paced multiplayer battles with up to 10 players
- Speed-based scoring (first correct answer gets most points)
- Real-time leaderboard updates during gameplay
- Position-based rewards (1st place gets most coins)
- Ranked and casual modes available

#### Daily Challenges (7 Unique Challenges)
- **Money Drop:** Answer questions before money drops away
- **Football Jeopardy:** Classic jeopardy-style board with categories
- **Clues:** Progressive clue reveals with decreasing points
- **True/False:** Quick-fire true or false statements
- **Emoji Guess:** Decode emoji sequences representing players/teams/events
- **Countdown:** Complete top-10 lists (e.g., "Top 10 goal scorers")
- **Put in Order:** Arrange items in correct chronological or numerical order

Each challenge resets 24 hours after completion with coin rewards.

#### Career Mode
- Single-player campaign with progressive difficulty
- Team selection and level-based progression
- Star rating system (1-3 stars based on performance)
- Unlockable levels and rewards
- Story-driven progression through football divisions

#### Historic Match Squad Builder
- Recreate winning lineups from famous matches
- Drag-and-drop player placement in formations
- Historical accuracy challenges
- Educational gameplay teaching football history
- Star-based completion rewards

### 2. Gamification & Progression

#### Tickets System
- 10 tickets per day (resets every 24 hours)
- 1 ticket required for ranked QuizBall or Buzzer Battle
- Daily challenges and career mode don't require tickets
- Purchase additional tickets via in-app purchases
- Premium users get unlimited tickets

#### Economy System
- **Coins:** Earned by winning matches, completing challenges, daily objectives
- **XP:** Earned alongside coins, contributes to level progression
- **Levels:** Visual representation of player experience (1-100+)
- **Streaks:** Consecutive win bonuses with fire emoji indicators

#### Objectives System
- **Daily Objectives:** Reset every 24 hours (e.g., "Win 3 QuizBall matches")
- **Weekly Objectives:** Reset every 7 days (e.g., "Complete all daily challenges")
- **Seasonal Objectives:** Long-term goals spanning months (e.g., "Reach Diamond rank")
- Claimable rewards (coins, XP, exclusive items)

#### Achievements & Badges
- 50+ achievements across categories:
  - Gameplay achievements (win streaks, perfect games)
  - Milestone achievements (100 games played, 1000 correct answers)
  - Special achievements (answer in < 1 second, beat top-ranked player)
  - Collection achievements (unlock all avatars in category)
- Tiered system: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum
- Display on profile as badges of honor

### 3. Social & Competitive Features

#### Leaderboards
- **Global Leaderboard:** Top 100 players worldwide
- **Country Leaderboard:** Top players in your country
- **Friends Leaderboard:** Compete with friends (future feature)
- Time-based filters (weekly, monthly, all-time)
- Real-time rank updates
- Player profiles with stats

#### Ranking System
- Starting rank: 1000 points
- Gain/lose rank points based on match results
- Rank divisions: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond → Legend
- Matchmaking based on similar rank (±200 points)
- Seasonal rank resets with rewards

### 4. Store & Monetization

#### Avatar Shop
- Customizable avatars with 3 components:
  - **Heads:** Player faces, footballs, trophies (emoji-based)
  - **Jerseys:** Team colors, national jerseys
  - **Accessories:** Hats, glasses, headbands
- 100+ unlockable items
- Rarity tiers: Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary
- Purchase with earned coins or real money

#### Premium Subscription
- **Benefits:**
  - 2x XP multiplier
  - 1.5x coins multiplier
  - Unlimited tickets
  - Exclusive premium avatars
  - Ad-free experience
  - Early access to new features
- Monthly and yearly plans with discount for annual

#### Tickets Shop
- Purchase ticket packages for immediate play
- Bundles: 10, 25, 50, 100 tickets with bonus amounts
- Premium users don't need to purchase (unlimited)

#### Rewards Wheel
- Free daily spin for random rewards
- Rewards include: coins, XP, tickets, rare avatars
- Additional spins purchasable with coins or real money
- Visual spinning animation with guaranteed win

### 5. Localization

- **English (EN):** Full localization

#### Localized Content

### 6. Real-Time Features

## Legal & Compliance

### Intellectual Property
- **Trademarks:** Trademark "QuizBall" name and logo
- **Question Content:** Ensure all questions are factual (public knowledge)
- **Image Rights:** Use only licensed or public domain images
- **Music/Sounds:** Use royalty-free or licensed audio

### Data Privacy
- **GDPR Compliance:** For European users
- **CCPA Compliance:** For California users
- **Data Protection:** Encryption, secure storage, minimal data collection
- **Privacy Policy:** Clear, comprehensive, accessible

### Terms of Service
- **User Agreement:** Define rules, prohibited behavior
- **Refund Policy:** Clear policy for in-app purchases
- **Content Moderation:** Policy for user-generated content (usernames, chat)

### Age Restrictions
- **COPPA Compliance:** No users under 13 (or require parental consent)
- **Age Verification:** Ask for birthdate during registration
- **Content Rating:** Rated for appropriate age group (PEGI 3, ESRB E)

### Gambling Regulations
- **Not Gambling:** Ensure no gambling mechanics
- **No Real-Money Prizes:** Keep all prizes virtual (coins, avatars)
- **Disclaimer:** Clear that it's a skill-based game, not chance
